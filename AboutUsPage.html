About Us

We began as simply as a group of friends who all had an interest in Dungeons and Dragons, we all began playing at roughly the same time and we thoroughly enjoyed our sessions. This shared interest in the game eventually lead to an idea to create a business surrounding our favourite game.
Our dnd dice retail company began as nothing special but with hard work and dedication it has changed from a simple idea to the multi million euro business it is today, with the 3 founders working hard in various areas of the business.

Cian Hughes - Financial Department Management
Darragh Mulvihill - Head of Product Development
Eoin Ocathasaigh - Head of Marketing & Explosives Manager

Even after all these years, with our lives getting ever busier with work and family we still find time to play dnd and simply relax. We hope our passion for the game and its many branches can spark even the smallest amount of interest in unfamiliar people and hopefully inspire them to simply give the game a try. Secret
